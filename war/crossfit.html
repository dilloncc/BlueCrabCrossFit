What is CrossFit

CrossFit is a fitness program for EVERYONE. Yep- everyone. From great-grandmothers to NFL Athletes, CrossFit can make any person into a better, healthier version of themselves. Using 'functional' movements, we teach you how to do things like squat, press, deadlift, swing, climb, and jump. The best thing is- you will never get bored! The workouts change every day. That is just the beginning- come try it out and see what CrossFit can do for you!
