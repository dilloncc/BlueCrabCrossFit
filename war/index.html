About

Elkridge, MD CrossFit. Central Maryland's Premier CrossFit facility. We are a 3,000 square foot facility located directly off of Rt. 1 at 6785 Business Parkway Elkridge, MD 21075. We use top-of-the-line Rouge Fitness equipment including barbells (mens, womens, and trainer), bumper plates, pullup rig, squat racks, ropes, tires, etc. Always, if you have any questions, please don't hesitate to call or email!
