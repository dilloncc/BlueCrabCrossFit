NEW TO CROSSFIT?

What is the first step?

Click HERE to schedule an intro session for free!

What to bring?

Yourself, athletic clothes/shoes, and a water bottle (we have a drinking fountain). WE take care of the rest!

What is next?

After the free intro session we will work to schedule your FOUNDATIONS class. This is a 4-class program that introduces you to the CrossFit movements in small-group atmosphere. When you "graduate" you will be ready to start attending any normal CrossFit class! Everyone graduates, and no, there is not a final exam!
